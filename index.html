Welcome to Kerala Tourism, where every moment is a journey into the heart of tranquility and beauty. Nestled in the lush landscapes of southern India, Kerala beckons with its enchanting backwaters, pristine beaches, and verdant hill stations. Explore the serene waterways of Alleppey and Kumarakom aboard traditional houseboats, or lose yourself in the misty tea plantations of Munnar and the untouched wilderness of Wayanad. Immerse yourself in the ancient healing traditions of Ayurveda, witness captivating Kathakali performances, and tantalize your taste buds with the flavors of Kerala cuisine. From thrilling wildlife safaris to cultural excursions and eco-adventures, Kerala offers a tapestry of experiences for every traveler. Come, discover the magic of Kerala with us and create memories that will last a lifetime.

TOP SPOTS

Munnar

Munnar, a picturesque hill station in Kerala, India, boasts lush tea gardens, mist-covered hills, breathtaking vistas, and abundant flora and fauna.

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach, a renowned coastal gem in Kerala, India, captivates with golden sands, azure waters, vibrant sunsets, and vibrant beach culture.

Lulu Kochi

LuLu Mall in Kochi is a sprawling shopping complex, with diverse retail outlets, entertainment options, dining choices, and recreational facilities.

Alleppey

Alleppey, known as the Venice of the East, enchants with its tranquil backwaters, scenic houseboat cruises, lush paddy fields, and vibrant culture.
